👪 About Repzilla
Here's what this app is all about.
🌱 A lifelong fitness foundation
Repzilla gives young athletes a place to set personalised fitness, lifestyle and health goals — habits they can carry with them throughout their lives. We believe that building a positive relationship with movement and exercise during youth is one of the greatest gifts a young person can receive.
💪 What your child will do
Log workouts • Track progress over time • Earn achievement badges • Stay motivated with body-positive messaging • Build healthy habits in a safe, supportive environment.
🛡️ Our commitment to safety
All content is age-appropriate. Our motivational messaging focuses exclusively on strength, effort, health and wellbeing — never appearance, weight or body size.
